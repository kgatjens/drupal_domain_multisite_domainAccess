Optim Spine

At Optim Spine, we have assembled a team of experts serving all areas of spine care—entirely under one roof.

We’re different in the way that we listen to our patients and think about and their problems – it’s evident from the first moment that we meet right through the process of getting you back to feeling good again.

We are a unique group of specialists and our expertise covers the full spectrum of spine care. With dedicated physical therapists, anesthesia and rehabilitation pain management specialists and surgeons with both orthopedic and neurosurgical training, our comprehensive team is like no other in Georgia. Our spine-focused network allows us to offer each patient the full spectrum of care including comprehensive non-operative strategies and the latest and most advanced minimally-invasive and reconstructive surgical treatments.

We understand the importance of offering specialized care to patients in both larger metropolitan areas and rural locations. We are conveniently found in over 20 satellite offices throughout southeast Georgia and have a state-of-the-art outpatient Surgery Center in Savannah, GA (Optim Surgery Center) and a full service Orthopedic specialty hospital located in Reidsville, GA (the physician-owned Optim Medical Center-Tattnall). Together, Optim Medical Center-Tattnall and Optim Surgery Center have exceptionally low complication rates. In 2013-2014 Optim’s spine surgery infection rate was 0.97% and 0% for pain management procedures.

Our spine patients are some of the most interesting and remarkable people that we encounter. Some have been dealing with pain for years while others have had recent injuries. Hear some of their experiences on our Patient Stories page.

For whatever spinal problems you may be having we hope that you will experience the Optim difference. Let Optim Spine help get you back to your pain-free self.

Optim Spine is a collaboration of Optim Orthopedics and the physician-owned Optim Medical Center-Tattnall.

Optim Primary Care-Tattnall Adds Roger Beville, MD Primary Care Physycian to Cobbtown and Glenville

share

REIDSVILLE, GA, October 1, 2015 –Optim Primary Care-Tattnall, a department of the physician-owned Optim Medical Center-Tattnall, adds Primary Care Physician Roger Beville, MD who will be seeing patients in Cobbtown and Glennville starting October 1, 2015. Dr. Beville will be joining Optim after most recently working for St. Joseph’s Candler Medical Group in Metter, GA.

Born and raised in Vinita, Oklahoma Dr. Beville lived in a few towns in Oklahoma and Missouri before moving to Georgia where he finished high school in Atlanta. He attended Georgia Southern University and completed Medical School at Medical College of Georgia in Macon, GA and Residency at Naval Regional Medical Center in Charleston, SC.

“I am really excited about Optim’s commitment to Primary Care and preventative medicine,” said Dr. Beville.

For more information about Roger Beville, MD please visitOptimHealth.com/RogerBeville and to make an appointment, please call 912.654.4599 (Glennville: 1000 B. North Veterans Blvd. • Glennville, GA 30427) and 912.684.2071 (Cobbtown: 36671 Hwy 23 • Cobbtown, GA 30420).

FRACTURE CARE BILLING

If we treat you or your dependent for a fracture, your insurance company requires that we bill our services to you using a coding system known as CPT (Current Procedure Terminology). The codes used to describe the services we completed for you are found in the “surgery” section of the CPT codebook. This does not mean that you had an operation. This is merely the way the CPT codebook is organized for ease of use by both insurance companies and physicians.

According to the CPT guidelines, fracture care is billed as a “packaged” service.

This means that at the time of initial care, a bill is generated that includes:

  • Treatment of the fracture 
  • The first cast or splint application 
  • 90 days of normal, uncomplicated, follow up care
